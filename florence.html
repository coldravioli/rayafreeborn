BONES

2023

48 x 8" Inkjet

A bit before turning 14, I bought myself a camera bag at a local camera shop. Surrounded by decaying film camera bags sat a vintage record labeled ‘Los Microwaves’. The album posed the question- is there life after breakfast? Intrigued and in awe of random nothingness, I began to seek projects loosely intertwined with food.

Ultimately, my hyperfixation with breakfast foods began with my first pancake, but was catalyzed by a brief encounter with the 80s vinyl.
